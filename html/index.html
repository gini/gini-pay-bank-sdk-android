Gini Pay Bank SDK for Android¶

Introduction¶

Gini Pay provides an information extraction system for analyzing business invoices and transfers them to the Android banking app, where the payment process will be completed. The Gini Pay Bank SDK for Android provides functionality to capture documents with mobile phones, accurate line item extraction enables the user to uncheck the items they don’t want to pay and automatically calculates the new amountToPay.
